About Dastaavej

Our Mission

Dastaavej was created with a simple mission: to make document tracking accessible, transparent, and efficient for every citizen. We understand the challenges people face when dealing with important documents like Passport and PAN Card, and we're here to simplify that process.

What We Do

Dastaavej provides a centralized platform for tracking your Passport and PAN Card applications. Our system allows you to:

  • Apply for new documents online
  • Track the status of your applications in real-time
  • Receive notifications at every stage of the process
  • Get support from our dedicated team when you need it
